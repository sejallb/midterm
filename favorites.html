FAVORITE RESTAURANTS

Maki Kosaka

Rating: 10/10 | Location: Chelsea

Maki Kosaka offers an exceptional sushi experience with fresh, high-quality ingredients and expert craftsmanship. The intimate setting and attentive service complement the standout omakase menu, showcasing delicate flavors and seasonal fish. A must-visit for sushi lovers.READ MORE →

COQODAQ

Rating: 10/10 | Location: Flatiron

COQODAQ offers a modern, vibrant dining experience with a focus on high-quality, flavorful dishes. Known for its creative menu, the restaurant blends international flavors with a unique twist, featuring fresh ingredients and artful presentations. The stylish ambiance and attentive service make it a great choice for those looking for a memorable meal in a lively setting.

GupShup

Rating: 9/10 | Location: Gramercy

GupShup delivers an exceptional dining experience with bold flavors that stand out. The Paneer Cheese Rolls, Tiger Prawns, Chicken Tikka, and Lucknowi Chicken Biryani are all delicious highlights. While the food is a bit pricey, the quality makes it worth the splurge.

Osteria Del Porto

Rating: 10/10 | Location: Financial District

Osteria Del Porto offers a charming Italian dining experience with a warm, welcoming atmosphere. The menu features classic Italian dishes made with fresh, high-quality ingredients, with standout pasta and seafood options. The attentive service and cozy ambiance make it a perfect spot for both casual meals and special occasions. A delightful choice for authentic Italian cuisine in the area.

Ippudo

Rating: 9.5/10 | Location: East Village

Ippudo is a fantastic spot for comfort ramen. My go-to is the Karaka Spicy Ramen, which is packed with bold, spicy, and delicious flavors that hit just right. The broth is rich and satisfying, perfect for when you're craving something hearty. The restaurant itself has a great ambience—cozy vibe. Ippudo offers a flavorful and comforting bowl of ramen in a welcoming setting. Definitely worth a visit if you're a ramen lover!
